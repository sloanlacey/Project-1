What is Earth's atmosphere made of?more_vert

Atmosphere: NASA

Card Titleclose

Earth's atmosphere is made up of roughly 78% nitrogen, 21% oxygen, and small amounts of other gases such as carbon dioxide, neon, and hydrogen.


How old is planet Earth?more_vert

Deep Time: NASA

Hint: it's old!close

Earth is roughly 4.5 billion years old, plus or minus about 50 million years. The age is determined by radiometrically dating the oldest rocks we can find.
