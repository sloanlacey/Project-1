What is Earth's atmosphere made of?more_vert

Atmosphere: NASA

A whole lot of gas.close

Earth's atmosphere is made up of roughly 78% nitrogen, 21% oxygen, and small amounts of other gases such as carbon dioxide, neon, and hydrogen. Our atmosphere acts like a protective shield that prevents us from being hit by incoming meteoroids by breaking them up before they can strike the surface.

How old is planet Earth?more_vert

Deep Time: NASA

Really, really old.close

Earth is roughly 4.5 billion years old, plus or minus about 50 million years. We have determined the age of Earth by radiometrically dating the oldest rocks we have discovered. The theory of the creation of the universe is in the Big Bang which sets the age of the universe itself at about 13.8 billion years. Billions of years later our solar system, including our home planet, was born.
