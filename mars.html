How big is the largest volcano in our solar system?more_vert

Mars: NASA

Strap in, it's enormous.close

Olympus Mons, a shield volcano, is located in the Tharsis Montes region of mars and is the largest volcano in our solar system. It has a diameter roughly the size of the state of Arizona, and is 16 miles high. Compared to Mt. Everest here on Earth, Olympus Mons is about two and a half times as large.

Is there water on Mars?more_vert

Water on Mars: NASA

Yes, as ice.close

Almost all of the water on Mars exists as ice, and a tiny percentage as water vapor in Mars' atmosphere. A massive quantity of water ice also exists beneath a permanent carbon dioxide ice cap at the Martian south pole. Liquid water has yet to be discovered on Mars, but it may have been present closer to when Mars first formed and was in closer proximity to our sun.
