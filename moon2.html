The Moon: NASA

Hint: it's not cheese!close

Rock and metal, just like planet Earth! The moon's surface is heavily cratered and is littered with dead volcanoes, as well as ancient lava flows--some of which can be seen with the naked eye. Beneath the surface is a potentially solid iron core, surrounded by an intermediate layer of molten liquid iron.
